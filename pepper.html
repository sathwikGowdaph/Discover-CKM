Pepper

Pepper is one of the important spice crops grown in Chikkamagaluru. It thrives in the shade of coffee plantations and is prized for its flavor and aroma.The main type of pepper cultivated and traded in the Chikkamagaluru district is Black Pepper, a staple spice in the region, with other forms like white, green, and pink peppers also available from dealers. Chikkamagaluru is a significant hub for pepper production in Karnataka, which itself is the largest producer of black pepper in India.

Pepper

Milder than black pepper, white pepper is used in light-colored dishes where its color is preferred for aesthetic reasons. Green Pepper: This fresh, fruity, and slightly spicy pepper is used in sauces and gourmet dishes. Pink Pepper: Though not a true pepper, this variety offers a sweet and floral flavor, popular for garnishes and adding a unique touch to dishes.
