Popular falls

kalahatti falls

According to local legends, the Kallathigiri Falls was a pilgrim centre of the Saint Agasthya. Agasthya Muni chanced upon this waterfall during his traveling and stayed here for a few months. Here, you can explore the Veerabhadra temple, that was constructed during the era of Vijayanagara

Shanti Falls

This isn't a great falls, when you compare it with Hebbe falls. It is on the way to Z-point. You can go by car or walk from the road to Z point,Shanti Falls Chikmagalur is open every day. You can visit it during the daytime, from 6 AM to 6 PM, when it offers good visibility.
